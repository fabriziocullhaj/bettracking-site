Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Bet Tracking collects, uses, and protects user personal data.

Data collected

  • Identification data (e.g. email) when the user creates an account or signs in.
  • Usage data entered by the user (e.g. bets, stats, preferences).
  • Technical data (e.g. device type, operating system).

Purpose of processing

  • Provide app features and sync data across devices.
  • Improve user experience and service quality.
  • Manage Premium subscriptions via official stores.
  • Show ads to free users (when available).

Third-party services

We use third-party services to provide the app, including:

  • Firebase (Google) for authentication and data sync.
  • AdMob (Google) for in-app advertising.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play for in-app purchases.

Data retention

Data is stored for as long as needed to provide the service. Users can request account deletion at any time; removal is immediate and final.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access or loss.

User rights

Users can request access, correction, or deletion of their data. For privacy requests:
